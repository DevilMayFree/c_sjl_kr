Our Firm

About
SJL Partners

SJL Partners is a private equity investment manager based in Seoul, Korea. The company was founded in 2017, seeking to create an investment platform focusing on outbound cross-border opportunities out of Korea and the Asia-Pacific.

We deliver a unique investment proposition to our investors by partnering with strategic parties to leverage the "best of both worlds". We combine SJL’s expertise in cross-border M&A execution, operational improvements, and governance with the ability of the strategic partner to bring compelling synergies and mitigate risks.

What We Do

We believe there are substantial value creation opportunities in cross-border M&As in OECD markets.

By investing together with strategic investors in Asia, we can make synergies more tangible and investment risks more controllable.

Our strength lies in finding such opportunities and realizing value from them.
